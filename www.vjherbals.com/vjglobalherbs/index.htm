About Us

We, V.J.Global Herbs was commenced in the year 2008, are indulged in manufacturing a wide array of Herbal Syrups and Hand Sanitizers, Herbal Juice, Herbal Powder, Siddha and Ayurvedic Medicine. The offered product range consists of Herbal Juice, Herbal Syrups, and Pharmaceutical Syrup. In addition to this, these products are widely supported by our customers for accurate composition, high purity, and long shelf life.
A quality guarantee is the main purpose of our business. We struggle hard to maintain our offered herbal syrups and hand sanitizersrange correctly. Also, we have advanced infrastructure equipment. This unit is split into manufacturing, finishing, quality testing, research & development, warehouse, and packaging. Furthermore, all processes are managed as per the specific need of the customers and with guidelines of the industry. Additionally, we have installed a spacious warehouse that is facilitated with all the essential tools that are required for safely storing our herbal syrups and hand sanitizers. 
Our mentor Mr. Gopal maintains close relations in the company to undertake bulk orders proficiently. He manages us to deliver exceptional quality herbal syrups and hand sanitizers to the patrons.+ Read More
